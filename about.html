About Me

Hello and welcome to my website!

My name is Amy, and I am an undergraduate student studying EECS.

As an EECS major, I have a passion for designing and creating innovative technology that can improve people's lives. My particular interest lies in the realm of frontend design and user interface, as I believe that a well-designed interface can greatly enhance the user experience and make technology more accessible to everyone.

Aside from my academic pursuits, I also enjoy spending my free time playing video games. Gaming has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember, and I find it to be a great way to unwind and relax after a busy day of coding and studying.

On this website, you will find a collection of my projects and designs, as well as some insights into my personal interests and hobbies. I hope you enjoy browsing through my work and getting to know me better!
