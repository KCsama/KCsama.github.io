Kippy
Help, meet, and care for your furry friend today!

Kippy is a web app that support the pet's parents along their journey with their pet friends.

Kippy is the final project for CS160. I worked on this web application with 5 other teammates. Kippy is an app built for pet owners specifically dogs and cats owners. Because raising pets are a huge responsibilities and a heavy duty, our purpose of designing this is to provide a platform where pet parents can meet with each other and share their valuable experiences about raising their pet. It is also a platform for the pet parents to securely contain medical informations about their pet. See full report here See sample video here
SunSight
Plan, View, and Meet Up with others.

A website designed for sightseers with the options of comparing trails, contacting other hikers, and filtered searching results.

This is a frontend application model built for hikers who are sightseers. This is a project for CS160. In this app, you can add trails to your planner. You can plan your trip ahead of time. You can also search using filters. You can also compare two different trails when planning your trip. When you receive alerts about people wanting to meet you, you can also take a look at their profile. You can also receive notifications when you reach a fork in your trail. See full report here.
